# Spa Images

Place your spa digital signage images in this directory.

## Required Images:

Based on your CSV data, you need these images:

- `Digitalsignage_spa.png` - General spa image
- `Digitalsignage_healthspa.png` - Health spa, Day spa, Resort spa, etc.
- `Digitalsignage_nailspa.png` - Nail spa image
- `Digitalsignage_ayurvedic.png` - Ayurvedic spa image  
- `Digitalsignage_yogameditationcenter.png` - Yoga & meditation centers
- `Digitalsignage_saunastudio.png` - Sauna studios
- `Digitalsignage_hotspringspa.png` - Hot spring spas
- `Corporate_Wellness_spa.png` - Corporate wellness centers

## Temporary Solution:

Until you add your actual images, you can use placeholder images from Unsplash or create simple branded graphics.

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px or larger
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:10 or 4:3
- **Theme**: Calming, professional spa environments
- **Style**: High quality, well-lit, inviting

## Quick Fix:

For testing, you can temporarily use Unsplash URLs in the location data until your images are ready.
