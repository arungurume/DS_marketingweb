## What is Digital Signage?

Digital signage is a form of electronic display that shows information, advertising and other messages. Digital signs (such as LCD, LED, plasma displays, or projected images) can be found in public spaces, transportation systems, museums, stadiums, retail stores, hotels, restaurants, and corporate buildings etc., to provide wayfinding, exhibitions, marketing and outdoor advertising.

In its simplest form, you can think of it as a digital version of a traditional poster or sign. But its capabilities go far beyond that.

## Why Your Business Needs It

In today's fast-paced world, grabbing customer attention is harder than ever. Traditional static signs are easily ignored. Digital signage, on the other hand, is dynamic, engaging, and can be updated in an instant.

<Callout>
  **Key takeaway:** 70% of people say digital signage is a compelling way to advertise, and 55% can recall the specific message from a digital billboard.
</Callout>

### Key Benefits
* **Increased Sales:** Promote high-margin products, run limited-time offers, and upsell customers with enticing visuals.
* **Enhanced Customer Experience:** Reduce perceived wait times with entertaining content and provide helpful information like wayfinding.
* **Instant Updates:** Change prices, update menus, and launch new promotions across all your locations with a few clicks. No more printing costs!
* **Improved Communication:** Share important announcements with employees in an office setting or with customers in a retail store.

## Getting Started: The Hardware

You might think you need expensive, specialized hardware to get started. But you probably already own most of what you need.

![A TV with a media player stick](https://picsum.photos/seed/bloginline1/800/500)
*A modern TV and a simple media player is all you need.*

The two core components are:
1.  **A Display:** Any modern TV with an HDMI port will work perfectly.
2.  **A Media Player:** This is a small device that plugs into your TV's HDMI port, connects to the internet, and plays the content you send it. Devices like the **Amazon Fire TV Stick** are incredibly powerful and affordable options.

## The Software: Your Content Management System (CMS)

The media player is the body, but the Content Management System (CMS) is the brain. A good CMS, like DigitalSigns.ai, is a cloud-based platform that lets you:

*   Create and design your content.
*   Upload images and videos.
*   Schedule when and where your content plays.
*   Manage hundreds of screens from a single dashboard.

Choosing the right CMS is the most critical part of your digital signage strategy. Look for one that is easy to use, reliable, and affordable.
